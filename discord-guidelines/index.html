Discord Guidelines

These guidelines explain what is and isn’t allowed within Roblox Troll Central. They exist to keep the community welcoming, safe, and enjoyable for everyone. Moderators may act if behavior disrupts the server or violates Discord’s policies.

Disrespect

Harassment, insults, targeting users, or being intentionally hostile toward others is not allowed.

Channel Misuse

Use channels only for their intended purpose and follow pinned messages or channel descriptions.

Other Languages

English should be used unless a channel explicitly allows other languages.

Brainrot

Excessive low-effort memes, spam phrases, or repetitive content that disrupts discussion is not allowed.

Misusing Bots

Do not abuse bots, commands, reactions, or automated systems.

Non-Pingable Names

Usernames or nicknames must be readable and pingable. Excessive symbols or blank names are not allowed.

Small Advertising

Indirect promotion, subtle ads, or links in names/profiles without permission are not allowed.

Drama

Do not start or bring drama into the server. Personal conflicts should be kept out of RTC.

Spamming

Message flooding, emoji spam, repeated messages, or excessive pinging is prohibited.

Disrupting Peace

Intentionally causing chaos, derailing conversations, or provoking others is not tolerated.

Death Wishes & Threats

Wishing harm, death, or threatening others — even as a joke — is strictly prohibited.

Sensitive Topics

Avoid discussions involving politics, religion, real-world violence, or other sensitive topics.

Advertising

Major advertising, DM advertising, or server promotions are not allowed without staff approval.

NSFW & Porn

Sexual content, pornography, or explicit material is not allowed under any circumstances.

Discrimination & Racism

Any form of discrimination, hate speech, or slurs is strictly forbidden.

Illegal Content

Content involving illegal activity, doxxing, hacking, or self-harm encouragement is prohibited.

Don't take us seriously — or anything

We are a trolling server. Anything within our server should not be taken seriously. We may moderate when it goes too far.

Discord Terms of Service

All users must comply with Discord’s Terms of Service and Community Guidelines at all times.

⚠️ These guidelines are not exhaustive. Staff may intervene in situations not explicitly listed. Attempting to exploit loopholes or argue semantics may result in moderation.
