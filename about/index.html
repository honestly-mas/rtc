About RTC

Roblox Troll Central, also known as RTC, is one of the fastest growing Roblox trolling communities. Our growth has been powered largely by our TikTok page and social platforms, bringing in thousands of users in a very short time.

RTC was created in late June and reached over 500 members within just three days of launch. As of now, we are approaching 900 members and continue to grow every day.

We host active events, maintain an engaged community, and provide a wide range of perks for our members.
