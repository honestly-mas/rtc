Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to the most common questions about Roblox Troll Central. If your question is not listed, our support team is always available.

How do I join the server?

You can join through our invite link: https://rblx.xyz/discord

How can I support RTC?

Simply joining our server helps a lot. You can also support us further by spending in our shop.

How do you leak guides?

Guides are sourced from our community and other private sources.

Can I partner with RTC?

Yes. If you have 100+ members and meet the requirements listed here: rblx.xyz/partners, you can open a ticket to apply.

I got banned. Can I appeal?

To appeal, please join our support server and follow the instructions to open a ticket.

Yes. Please confirm your attendance by reacting in the event channel in our Discord. We aim to host weekly events. Sign-up is not required — you can still join when the event starts.
