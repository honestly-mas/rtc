Trial Moderation

The following is not deemed confidential and is public.

Congratulations on passing your staff application and becoming a Trial Moderator. This stage is designed to evaluate your activity, judgment, and professionalism within the RTC community.

How to Pass

  • Be active and visible within the server
  • Show your moderation skills clearly and consistently
  • Moderate regularly and fairly
  • Engage with the community in a positive manner
  • Ask questions if you are unsure about a situation

Commands

The command prefix is !. Trial moderators have access to the following commands:

!warn <user> <reason> !mute <user> <time> <reason> !modlogs <user>

Guidelines

  • Follow and regularly re-read all RTC policies: Terms of Service, Discord Guidelines, Punishment Guide
  • You must be present in the support server: rblx.xyz/support
  • Remain active in public channels
  • Moderate regularly and consistently
  • If a punishment is out of your reach (kick or ban), ask a Manager+ in trial chat
  • Always double-check the punishment guide before acting
  • Maintain professionalism at all times
  • You are advised (but not required) to attend RTC events
Trial Moderation is a review period. Consistent performance, good judgment, and respectful behavior will significantly increase your chances of promotion.
